BETA

Explanations

  • This page is work in progress.
  • This tier list is generated automatically.
  • It works by parsing the character kit and generating a general score accordingly. It is then ranked alongside every other characters.
  • Which means it rates what the character offers, not its usage in specific scenarios such as FA, OTK, etc...
  • You can sort by clicking on the headers.
  • You can filter by name, element and more (Example: "female fire grand draph").
  • Ratings in a nutshell:
    • Core: Must have characters.
    • Good: Simply good to have characters.
    • Niche: Might be useful in certain scenarios or if you lack better characters.
    • Trash: Probably not worth your time.
